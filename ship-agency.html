Our vast shipping agency experience in Central Africa ensures that we continuously dedicate our time to providing the best agency services for container, break-bulk, oil & gas sectors, including tramp vessels and liner services in Douala,Limbe andKribi ports, Cameroon.

With a proven track record in the shipping arena Global Logistics and Trading Company Sarl today is a trusted partner to many ship owners, charterers and operators in the region.

With a value added chain of strong relationships with concerned authorities, be it ports or customs, documentations and on-line accessibility, the manner of our operations will assure you of our commitment to your business.

GLTC's experienced Port Agency teams have local knowledge of the respective areas and offer 24/7 one-point-of-contact services to all types of vessels including ship supply services.

Central Africa is a region of great economic potential, and with ongoing significant investments in ports and infrastructure, international businesses are looking at Central Africa to grow their business.

Emerging markets present their own unique challenges, and charterers, vessel operators and owners need an experienced and knowledgeable safe pair of local hands to ensure port calls proceed as smoothly and efficiently as possible.

Additionally, we offer Port Agency Together with our extensive Marine Logistics services; this makes us a leading player in the marine industry.

We create specialized solutions always in close cooperation with our clients.

THE SHIP AGENCY SERVICES WE OFFER INCLUDE :
- Liner agency
- Full ship's agency for all vessel types
- Vessel husbandry
- Protective Agency
- Bunkers
- Ship to Ship (STS) Operations

People are our key resource. Prompt and clear communications, combined with operational know-how and unsurpassed local knowledge & expertise, enables us to respond quickly and efficiently to our customers' needs.

For more detailed information of the services offered please visit or Contact us for quotations and more information.
